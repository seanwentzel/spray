Cellular Automata Puzzle

Welcome to the Cellular Automata Puzzle! Your goal is to figure out the hidden rules of a cellular automaton.

You need to create an initial pattern (top row) that will evolve into the target pattern (bottom row) after several generations. Each cell's state in the next generation depends on its current state and the states of its neighbors according to hidden rules.

Click on cells in the input row to toggle them between alive (black) and dead (white). Then click "Evolve" to see how your pattern evolves under the hidden rules.
